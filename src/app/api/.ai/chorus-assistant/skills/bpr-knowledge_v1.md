# BPR Knowledge — Quality Management System

Domain knowledge about the CHUV Sponsor Research Office (BPR) Quality Management System.

## What is the BPR?

The **Bureau du Promoteur de Recherche (BPR)** is the Sponsor Research Office at CHUV, Lausanne. It manages the Quality Management System (QMS) governing clinical trials and human research under Swiss and international regulations.
